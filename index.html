iPhone / iPad
Get TrackFolks on iOS
Available via TestFlight — Apple's official beta testing platform.
Open in TestFlight
1
Install TestFlight from the App Store (free, by Apple).
2
Tap "Open in TestFlight" above, or visit testflight.apple.com/join/Q4qpE2eh on your iPhone.
3
Tap Accept → Install inside TestFlight.
4
Open TrackFolks and sign in with your registered phone number.
Android
Get TrackFolks for Android
Download and install the APK directly — no Play Store required.
Download APK
1
Tap Download APK above and wait for the download to complete.
2
Open your Downloads folder and tap the trackfolks.apk file.
3
If prompted, go to Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps and allow your browser to install apps.
4
Tap Install and open TrackFolks to sign in.
Note: You may see a "Play Protect" warning. Tap Install anyway — TrackFolks is safe and distributed privately for your organisation.
