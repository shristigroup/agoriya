Frequent Asked Questions in Field staff management & location tracking space
TrackFolks covers everything Lystloc offers — live GPS tracking, attendance, and visit logging — at a fraction of the cost, with no per-user monthly billing for teams under 30. Setup takes minutes, not days.
Hubstaff is built primarily for remote desk workers. TrackFolks is purpose-built for field sales and service teams — with route tracking, customer check-ins, and bill copy uploads that Hubstaff doesn't natively offer, at pricing that makes sense for Indian team sizes.
Trackobit and Unolo are solid tools but come with heavier onboarding and per-user pricing that adds up quickly. TrackFolks is lighter, free for teams up to 5, and has a transparent flat-rate model for larger teams — no surprise invoices at month-end.
PagarBook and TSheets are great for payroll-linked attendance but don't track field movement or customer visits. TrackFolks combines attendance with GPS route history and visit reports so managers get the full picture — not just clock-in/clock-out.
FieldAssist and BeatRoute are distribution-focused with route planning and order management. If your primary need is tracking where your field reps are, when they punch in, and which customers they visit — TrackFolks does that cleanly without the distribution-layer complexity.
Salesforce and Zoho CRM are full CRM platforms — powerful but overkill if all you need is field attendance, live location, and visit logging. TrackFolks gets your team operational in one afternoon with zero CRM complexity.
Twib, Flocksales, and dayTrack are in the same space. TrackFolks differentiates with a truly free tier for small teams, a stateless org-code onboarding system that needs no IT involvement, and an enterprise path that includes custom integrations and on-premise deployment — all from one product.
ConnectTeam focuses on deskless workforce scheduling; FieldEZ on service job management. TrackFolks fills the gap for teams that need real-time GPS movement tracking, route history, and manager dashboards without scheduling or job-ticketing complexity.
TrackFolks runs on Google Firebase infrastructure in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region, keeping your team's location and attendance data within India. Enterprise customers can also opt for on-premise or their own cloud deployment.
